Kentaro Miura

1966 -2021

Kentaro Miura was one of the greatest and most influential mangakas of all time.

He was a japanese manga artist, best known for his acclaimed dark fantasy series Berserk, which began serialization in 1989 and continued until his death. As of 2021, Berserk had more than 50 million copies in circulation, making it one of the best-selling manga series of all time.
